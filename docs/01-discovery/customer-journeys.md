# Customer Journeys

## Player onboarding

1. Register account
2. Verify email/phone
3. Complete profile
4. Submit KYC documents
5. Activation and login

## Wallet and payments

1. Deposit funds
2. Place bets (casino or sports)
3. Track balance and bonus wagering
4. Withdraw funds

## Bonus usage

1. View available bonuses
2. Claim/activate bonus
3. Wager on eligible games
4. Bonus converts or forfeits

## Responsible gambling

1. Configure limits
2. Cooling-off period
3. Self-exclusion
4. Reality check notifications

## Admin operations

1. Search and review player accounts
2. Approve KYC or apply restrictions
3. Manage bonuses and campaigns
4. Review payments and refunds
5. Monitor reporting and analytics
