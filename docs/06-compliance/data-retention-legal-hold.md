# Data Retention and Legal Hold - Anjouan

## Regulatory requirement

- Business transaction records tied to suspicious transaction reports SHALL be retained for six years after termination of the business transaction (Money Laundering Act 008 of 2005, section 12(j)).
- Business transaction records include identification of parties, transaction purpose, account details, and total value (Money Laundering Act 008 of 2005, definitions).

## Data categories (to define)

- Player profile and KYC documents
- Financial transactions and wallet ledger
- Bonus and wagering history
- Audit logs and security events

## Open items

- The Computer Gaming Licensing Act 007 of 2005 does not state explicit retention periods for non-reported transactions; confirm KYC and transaction retention requirements with the Gaming Board.
