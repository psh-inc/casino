# Regulatory Sources Register - Anjouan (Comoros)

This register records the authoritative sources used to derive compliance requirements. Sources were retrieved online from the Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority and Anjouan Gaming Board sites.

## Primary sources

1) Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority Act 003 of 2005 (Establishment and duties of the Authority)
- URL: https://anjouanoffshorefinanceauthority.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/7.pdf
- Notes: Establishes the Authority and its duties.

2) Money Laundering (Prevention) Act 008 of 2005
- URL: https://anjouanoffshorefinanceauthority.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/8.pdf
- Notes: Defines identification records, business transaction records, and suspicious transaction reporting.

3) International Business Companies Act 004 of 2005
- URL: https://anjouanoffshorefinanceauthority.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/2.pdf
- Notes: Governs incorporation and operation of IBCs, which commonly hold licenses.

4) International Business Companies Regulations 004 of 2005
- URL: https://anjouanoffshorefinanceauthority.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/3.pdf
- Notes: Implements the IBC Act.

5) General Gaming License Conditions (Gaming Board requirements)
- URL: https://anjouanoffshorefinanceauthority.org/general-gaming-license-conditions/
- Notes: Operational requirements for gaming licensees; references the Computer Gaming Licensing Act 007 of 2005.

6) Computer Gaming Licensing Act 007 of 2005 (Online Gaming Act)
- URL: https://anjouangaming.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/Gaming-act-2005.pdf
- Notes: Core licensing, account maintenance, deposit/withdrawal rules, offences, and Board powers.
